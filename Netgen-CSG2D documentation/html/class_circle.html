TopoDS_Shape Circle::MakeCircle ( )

Creates a circle represented as a bounded surface in the 3D space.

It takes the edge of the circle, adds it to a wire and eventually, creates a face resulted from taking the closed area determined by the wire on a bounded surface. It returns the face resulted.
